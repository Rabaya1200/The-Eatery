Our Story

Discover Our Culinary Journey

Founded in 2005 by Chef Michael Laurent, The Eatery has been redefining fine dining in Dhaka. Our restaurant combines traditional techniques with modern innovation to create unforgettable dining experiences.

We pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farmers and artisans, ensuring each dish tells a story of quality and passion. Our seasonal menu changes regularly to reflect the best produce available.

Our elegant dining room features handcrafted furnishings and a warm, inviting atmosphere perfect for any occasion - from intimate dinners to celebratory gatherings.
